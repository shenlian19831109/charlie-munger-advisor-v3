# Charlie Munger Advisor v3

> **"I'm rational. I only want to know where I will die, so I will never go there."**

Charlie Munger's Multi-Dimensional Thinking Framework for Investment, Business, and Life Decisions

---

## 🎯 Problem We Solve

In a world of information overload and cognitive biases, making sound decisions is increasingly difficult. Most people rely on single perspectives, emotional reactions, or herd mentality—leading to poor investment choices, flawed business strategies, and regrettable life decisions. The challenge is not having more information, but having a **robust mental framework** to process it rationally.

## ✨ Our Solution: The Charlie Munger Advisor Skill

This AI-driven skill embodies Charlie Munger's 60+ years of investment wisdom and thinking frameworks. It provides a structured approach to analyzing complex problems through:

- **Multi-Dimensional Thinking Models** (Lattice Theory)
- **Psychological Biases & Incentive Analysis** (25 Cognitive Errors)
- **Investment Philosophy** (Moats, Circle of Competence, Margin of Safety)
- **Reverse Thinking & Long-term Rationality**

## 💡 Key Features & Benefits

- **Dual Language Support**: Both Chinese and English versions for global accessibility
- **Comprehensive Thinking Framework**: Guides users through Munger's core mental models:
  1. **Reverse Thinking**: "Where will I die? Never go there."
  2. **Circle of Competence**: "Do I truly understand this?"
  3. **Margin of Safety**: "Is the price low enough?"
  4. **Economic Moats**: "Why can't competitors replicate this?"
  5. **Incentive Analysis**: "What behaviors does this system reward?"
  6. **Lollapalooza Effect**: "Are multiple positive factors compounding?"
  7. **Long-term Thinking**: "Will this decision hold in 10 years?"
  8. **Rationality Check**: "Am I deceiving myself?"

- **Knowledge Graph Integration**: Structured representation of Munger's thinking patterns, relationships, and decision frameworks
- **Interactive Visualization**: D3-based knowledge graph for exploring connections between concepts
- **Actionable Outputs**: Delivers clear analysis, decision frameworks, and risk assessments
