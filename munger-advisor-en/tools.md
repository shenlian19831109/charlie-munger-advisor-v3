# Charlie Munger Tool Templates

## 1. Investment Decision Checklist

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│       Munger Investment Decision Checklist        │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

【Business Quality Assessment】

□ 1. Is this a good business?
    - ROE > 15%?
    - Has moat? What type?
      □ Supply-side economies of scale
      □ Demand-side network effects
      □ Brand premium
      □ Regulation / Franchises
      □ Switching costs
    - Will this business still exist in 10 years?

□ 2. Is the moat sustainable?
    - Will moat be wider or narrower in 5 years?
    - Will tech change threaten the moat?
    - Can competitors copy it?

□ 3. Can competitors replicate your advantage?
    - How sustainable is the first-mover advantage?

【Management Assessment】

□ 4. Is management rational?
    - Incentives aligned with shareholders?
    - History of over-aggressive behavior?
    - Do they do the right thing in tough times?

□ 5. Is management honest?
    - Transparent disclosures?
    - History of accounting problems?
    - Candid with shareholders?

□ 6. Is management capable?
    - Operating within their circle of competence?
    - Reasonable capital allocation decisions?
    - Know when to exit?

【Price Assessment】

□ 7. Is price reasonable?
    - PE/PB/PS at what point in historical range?
    - Expensive or cheap vs peers?
    - Intrinsic value vs current price discount?

□ 8. Is margin of safety sufficient?
    - Price >30% below intrinsic value?
    - Can I survive worst case?
    - Downside limited, upside unlimited?

□ 9. Is this my best opportunity?
    - Opportunity cost: what else could I invest in?
    - Better than what I currently hold?

【Circle of Competence】

□ 10. Do I truly understand this business?
    - What are the core drivers?
    - What are the main risks?
    - Can I describe the business in one sentence?
    - If not, don't own it

【Inversion】

□ 11. Where would I die?
    - What would make this investment fail?
    - Worst case: loss amount? Probability?
    - Can I survive it?

□ 12. Does Lollapalooza effect exist?
    - Multiple positive factors stacked?
    - Single factor or combination effect?
    - Can factors mutually reinforce?

□ 13. Am I investing or gambling?
    - Based on research or feeling?
    - Based on fundamentals or charts?
    - "I want it to go up" or "It should go up"?

【Decision Quality】

□ 14. Is my judgment rational?
    - Affected by emotions? (Fear / Greed / Hope)
    - Affected by social proof? ("Everyone's buying")
    - Affected by endowment? (Reluctant to sell)
    - Overconfidence?

□ 15. Will this decision hold in 10 years?
    - If I knew 10 years from now, would I invest now?
    - What will the business look like in 10 years?
    - Will my investment thesis still hold?

---

□ I have confidence in this investment
□ I have concerns about this investment (explain)
□ I decide not to invest (explain)

## 2. Life Decision Checklist

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│       Munger Life Decision Checklist              │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

【Goal Clarity】

□ 1. What do I want?
    - Precise description: not "I want success" but specific, measurable
    - Is this what I truly want or what society wants?
    - How will I view this in 5, 10, 20 years?

□ 2. Why do I want this?
    - Ask "why" 5 times
    - Ultimate reason: intrinsic or extrinsic motivation?

□ 3. Does this align with my core values?
    - Would I still do it if everyone disagreed?
    - Is this "I want" or "I should want"?

【Incentives】

□ 4. What are my incentives?
    - What drives current actions?
    - Do short-term incentives align with long-term goals?
    - What does my company/family/culture incentivize?

□ 5. Will this decision change my incentives?
    - Will it make me better or worse?
    - Will incentives change over time?

【Inversion】

□ 6. Where would I die?
    - What behaviors/decisions lead to life tragedy?
    - Does this decision increase or decrease those risks?
    - What "never do" list should I build?

□ 7. What is the worst case?
    - Worst possible outcome
    - Probability of occurrence
    - Can I survive it?

□ 8. Will I regret this decision in 10 years?
    - If still right in 10 years, probably right now
    - If I can't sleep because of this decision, something's wrong

【Circle of Competence】

□ 9. What is my circle of competence?
    - What am I truly good at?
    - What do I have real insight into?
    - What do I know I don't know?

□ 10. Is this decision within my circle?
    - Do I have real understanding of this area?
    - Am I relying on luck or skill?
    - Do I know what I don't know?

【Opportunity Cost】

□ 11. What is the opportunity cost?
    - What am I giving up by making this decision?
    - What else could I do with same time/money/energy?
    - Best choice or settling?

□ 12. If this were my last decision, how would I choose?
    - If this were the only opportunity, what would I choose?
    - If not, am I settling?

【Psychology Check】

□ 13. Am I thinking rationally or reacting emotionally?
    - Fear? Greed? Anger? Self-pity? Social proof?
    - Are these emotions affecting my judgment?

□ 14. Who am I comparing myself to?
    - Am I living my life or others' lives?
    - "Everyone does this" is not a reason

□ 15. Am I investing in the future or compensating for the past?
    - Looking forward or backward?
    - Is sunk cost affecting my judgment?

---

□ I have confidence in this decision
□ I have concerns (explain)
□ I decide not to do this (explain)

## 3. Business Analysis Checklist

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│       Munger Business Analysis Checklist         │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

【Moat Analysis】

□ 1. What is this company's moat?
    □ Supply-side economies of scale
      - High fixed costs? Marginal cost ~zero per additional customer?
      - Bigger scale = lower cost?
    
    □ Demand-side network effects
      - More users = better product?
      - Winner-take-all market?
    
    □ Brand
      - Willingness to pay premium for brand?
      - Emotional connection?
    
    □ Regulation / Franchise
      - License? Patent? Exclusive permit?
      - Hard for competitors to enter?
    
    □ Switching costs
      - High cost to switch to competitor?
      - Used to it, hard to change?

□ 2. Will moat widen or narrow?
    - 5 years from now stronger or weaker?
    - Will tech change threaten moat?

【Economic Moat (Quantitative)】

□ 3. ROE > 15%?
    - Average ROE past 5 years
    - ROE trend: rising or falling?

□ 4. ROIC > WACC?
    - Investment returns exceed capital costs?
    - Creating or destroying value?

□ 5. Free cash flow positive?
    - Operating cash flow > capital expenditures?
    - Cash flow covers debt?

□ 6. Profit margin trend?
    - Expanding or compressing?
    - Is competition driving down prices?

【Competitive Landscape】

□ 7. Industry competition structure?
    - "Blue ocean" or "Red ocean"?
    - Price war or differentiation?
    - "Civil war" in industry?

□ 8. Main competitors?
    - Their moat vs this company's moat?
    - Competition intensifying or easing?

□ 9. Potential disruptors?
    - New entrants? New tech? New business models?
    - Can moat withstand disruption?

【Management Assessment】

□ 10. Is management rational?
    - Historical capital allocation reasonable?
    - Do right thing in tough times?
    - Incentives aligned with shareholders?

□ 11. Is management honest?
    - Transparent disclosures?
    - History of accounting/governance issues?
    - Willing to admit mistakes?

□ 12. Is management capable?
    - Operating within circle of competence?
    - Clear strategy?
    - Strong execution?

【Risk Assessment】

□ 13. What are the main risks?
    - Top 3 risks listed
    - Probability of each?
    - Impact of each?

□ 14. Worst case?
    - What happens if all risks occur simultaneously?
    - Can company survive?
    - Can investors survive?

□ 15. Margin of safety?
    - Price discount to intrinsic value?
    - Even conservative estimates reasonable?

【Long-term Assessment】

□ 16. What will industry look like in 10 years?
    - Industry growing or shrinking?
    - How will tech change industry?
    - Will this company be stronger or weaker?

□ 17. Does management have adaptability?
    - How have they responded to change historically?
    - R&D/innovation culture?
    - Learn from mistakes?

---

□ This company has investment value
□ This company has no investment value (explain)
□ Need more information (list what)
```

## 5. Misjudgment Tendency Self-Check

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│       Munger Misjudgment Tendency Self-Check     │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

Before making any major decision, check if you're affected by:

【Reward/Punishment Super-Reaction】
□ Am I ignoring long-term consequences for short-term rewards?
□ What wrong decisions would these incentives cause?

【Social Proof】
□ Am I doing something because "everyone's doing it" or because it's right?
□ Am I thinking independently or following the crowd?

【Optimism】
□ Am I overestimating chances of success?
□ Can I survive worst case?

【Loss Aversion】
□ Am I avoiding the right thing because I fear loss?
□ Losing $100 hurts twice as much as gaining $100 helps, can I accept that?

【Endowment】
□ Am I valuing something higher just because I own it?
□ If I didn't have it, would I buy it?

【Self-Protection】
□ Am I making excuses for past mistakes?
□ Can I admit "I was wrong"?

【Over-Reflection】
□ Am I looking for causes in the wrong places?
□ Is this problem within my control?

【Association】
□ Am I ignoring problems because "this person/company is good"?
□ Does A happening guarantee B will follow?

【Willpower Depletion】
□ Is my decision-making affected by fatigue?
□ Should I reconsider when I'm fresh?

【Time Perception Distortion】
□ Am I overvaluing now vs future?
□ Is delayed gratification right?

【Stress-Induced Misjudgment】
□ Am I under stress right now?
□ Decisions under stress are usually bad decisions

【Self-Justification】
□ Am I finding reasons to justify my decisions?
□ Pre-deciding then finding reasons is dangerous

---

【Summary】
- Which tendencies might be affecting me?
- What should I do to avoid these misjudgments?
```
